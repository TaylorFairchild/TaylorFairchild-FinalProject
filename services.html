Offensive Legends

Offensive Legends have abilities that are useful in combat.

Wraith: Interdimensional Skirmisher

    Passive

  • Voices form the Void: A warning that triggers when an enemy is aiming at you or when there are traps nearby.

    • Tactical

  • Into the Void: Reposition quickly through the safety of the viod space, granting invincibility while leaving bright trails in your path.

    • Ultimate

  • Dimensional Rift: Wraith links 2 locations with portals for 60 seconds, allowing everyone to travel between the two points. Your tactical ability can be used at the same time.

Bangalore: Professional Soldier

    Passive

  • Double Time: Taking fire while sprinting makes you move faster for a brief time.

    • Tactical

  • Smoke Launcher: Fire 2 high-velocity smoke canisters that explodes into a smoke wall on impact, dealing 10 damage to near by enemeis.

    • Ultimate

  • Rolling Thunder: Call in an artillery strike that slowly creeps across the landscap dealing 40 damage with each explosion.

Mirage: Holographic trickster

    Passive

  • Now You See Me: Automatically go invisable when reviving teamates or using respawn beacons. A decoy is also spawned and you go invisable when downed.

    • Tactical

  • Psyche Out: Send out a holographic decoy to confuse the enemy.

    • Ultimate

  • Life of The Party: Deploy a team of 5 controllable decoys to distract enemies

Octane: High-speed Daredevil

    Passive

  • Swift Mind: Automatically restores heath over time.

    • Tactical

  • Stim: Increase walk speed by 30% and sprint by 40% for 6 seconds but it costs health to use.

    • Ultimate

  • Launch Pad: Deploy a jump pad that catapults anyone through the air. Press the jump button while in air to double jump.

Revenant: Synthetic Nightmare

    Passive

  • Stalker: You can crouch walk faster and climbs walls higher.

    • Tactical

  • Silence: Throw a ball that deals damage and disables enemy abilities for 15 seconds.

    • Ultimate

  • Death Totem: Drop a totem that protects users from death for 100 health. Instead of getting killed or downs you return to the totem when the 100 health is used.

Horizon: Gravitational Manipulator

    Passive

  • Spacewalk: Increases air control and reduces fall impacts so you don't experience the landing animation.

    • Tactical

  • Gravity Lift: Reverses the flow of gravity, lifting players upward and boosting them forward when they exit.

    • Ultimate

  • Black Hole: Deploy a device that creates a micro black hole pulling players towards it for 12 seconds.

Fuse: Explosives Enthusiast

    Passive

  • Grenadier: Stack an extra gernade per slot in your inventory. You can also fire grenades farther, faster, and more accurately.

    • Tactical

  • Knuckle Cluster: Launch a cluster bomb that continously explodes for 6 seconds.

    • Ultimate

  • The Motherlode: Launch a bombardment that encircles a target with a wall of fire. The fire causes 35 damage when walked through and continues to damage the target for 5 seconds after.

Ash: Incisive Instigator

    Passive

  • Marked for Death: Use deathboxes to show the locations of the players who killed them on your map.

    • Tactical

  • Arc Snare: Throw a spinning snare that damages and tethers the first enemy that get near.

    • Ultimate

  • Phase Breach: Tear open a one-way portal to a targeted location that lasts for 15 seconds.

Mad Maggie: Rebel Mad Maggie: Rebel Warlord

    Passive

  • Warlord's Ire: Temporarily highlight enemies you've damaged. You also run faster when holding shotguns.

    • Tactical

  • Riot Drill: Fire a drill that attaches to an obstacle and deals damage to enemies on either side of it.

    • Ultimate

  • Wrecking Ball: Throw a ball that releases speed boosting pads and blows up when near enemies.
