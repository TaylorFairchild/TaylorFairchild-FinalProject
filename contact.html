Defensive Legends

Defensive Legends can provide cover or fortify an area, making it safer for their squad.

Wattson: Static Defender

    Passive

  • Spark of Genius: An ultimate accelerant fully charges Wattson's ultimate and she can carry more. Wattson also slowly restores her sheilds over time.

    • Tactical

  • Perimeter Security: Create electric fences bt connecting nodes. Fences deal 20 damage and slow enemies for 3 seconds when they pass through them.

    • Ultimate

  • Interception Pylon: Place an electrfied pylon that destroys incoming ordnance and repairs up to 250 damaged sheilds between all legends.

Gibraltar: Sheilded Fortress

    Passive

  • Gun Sheild: Aiming down sight deploys a gun shield on Gibraltar's arm that blocks up to 50 damage of incoming fire.

    • Tactical

  • Dome of Protection: Gibraltar throws down a dome that blocks incoming and outgoing attacks that lasts for 12 seconds.

    • Ultimate

  • Defensive Bombardment: Call in a concentrated mortar strike on a marked position, dealing 40 damage per hit.

Caustic: Toxic Trapper

    Passive

  • Nox Vision: You gain threat vision on enemies moving through your gas.

    • Tactical

  • Nox Gas Trap: Place up to 6 canisters that release deadly Nox gas when shot or triggered by enemies. Each tick deals 5 damage and goes up by 1 for every other tick ex: 5, 5, 6, 6, 7, 7.

    • Ultimate

  • Nox Gas Grenade: Throw a grenade that explodes in a large blanket of gas. The damage is the dame as your canisters.

Rmpart: Base of Fire

    Passive

  • Modded Loader: Increased magazine capacity and faster relaods when using certain guns along with your ultimate gun.

    • Tactical

  • Amped Cover: Build a wall that's base takes up to 400 damage while the top is an amped wall that you can shoot through but blocks incoming shots for up to 175 damage.

    • Ultimate

  • Mobile Minigun: Wield a mobile minigun with a single high capacity magazine. Place the gun down to allow anyone to use it as a sationary and reloadable turrent.

Newcastle: Mobile Guardian

    Passive

  • Retrieve the Wounded: Drag downed allies as you revive them and protect them with your revive shield.

    • Tactical

    • Ultimate

Catalyst: Defensive Conjurer

    Passive

  • Barricade: Reinforce doors, strengthening them and locking them to enemeis. You can also create doors where doors have been destroyed. Reinforced doors take 4 kicks to break, double non-reinforced doors.

    • Tactical
