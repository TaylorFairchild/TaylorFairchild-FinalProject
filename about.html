Support Legends

Support Legends can aid their squad by providing healing or loot.

Lifeline: Combat Medic

    Passive

  • Combat Revive: Deploy D.O.C. to revive teamates, allowing Lifeline to defend.

    • Tactical

  • D.O.C. Heal Drone: The Drone Of Compassion (DOC) automatically heals 8 health per second to those near it for 20 seconds.

    • Ultimate

  • Care Package: Call in a care package full of high quality defense gear.

Loba: Translocating Thief

    Passive

  • Eye For Quality: Nearby epic and legendary loot can be seen through walls.

    • Tactical

  • Burglar's Best Friend: Teleport to a hard-to-reach place or escape trouble by throwing your jump drive bracelet.

    • Ultimate

  • Black Marget Boutique: Place a portable device that allows you to teleport 2 peices of nearby loot to your inventory.
